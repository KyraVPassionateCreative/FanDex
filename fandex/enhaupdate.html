ENHYPEN MEMBERS TEST POSITIVE FOR COVID-19

Another member from the group, ENHYPEN has tested positive for Covid-19. The band’s agency, Belift shared the health update on July 22 and said that the test result of Heesung has come back positive. As per the statement, his symptoms are not severe and only include a mild cold. Sharing the update, the agency wrote, “Hello. This is BELIFT LAB. We would like to provide you with some information on ENHYPEN member HEESEUNG'S COVID-19 confirmation and adjustments in his activities.”

“HEESEUNG tested positive from a rapid antigen test on the 22nd (Friday). He is currently showing symptoms like a mild cold and is being treated at home away from the other members,” the statement added. As a result of this, he will not be participating in any of the schedules for a while. His health will be closely monitored so that he can recover well. Before Heesung, another member, Jay also tested positive just a day ago. It was shared on July 21, that his rapid antigen test confirmed that Jay had contracted the virus. Both of them are currently resting.

As for the other members, their tests came out negative. All seven of them are living separately in solo residences. “All ENHYPEN members have been staying separately at a single-person residence, away from one another since the 21st (Thursday),” confirmed the agency. Furthermore, the results of the self-kit testing done by members Jungwon, Sunoo, and NI-KI have been reported to be negative, and member Sunghoon who also underwent a quick antigen test ahead of his schedule as an MC for KBS' music show, Music Bank, received a negative result. Meanwhile on July 4, 2022, ENHYPEN released their third mini-album, MANIFESTO: DAY 1, which included the title track "Future Perfect (Pass the Mic). They have been promoting it. The two members will not be participating in any of the activities for a while but will resume as soon as they are deemed fit and all rested.
